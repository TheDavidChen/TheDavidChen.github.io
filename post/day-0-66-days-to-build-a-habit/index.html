Day 0 - 66 Days To Build A Habit

Date Posted: May 31, 2020

66 days.

In a 2009 study by Lally et al. (2009), researchers found that their study of 96 individuals took on average 66 days to truly form a habit. While the amount of time varied from 18 to 254 days, 66 gives us a definitive number to work towards.

Change has, and always will be difficult. It takes dedication, intention, and a commitment to breaking old habits, even when it becomes painful.

66 days.

Over the next 66 days, we’ll work on one post a day. One meaningful post, related to the purpose of this website.

66 days.

It is day 0, and our journey has begun.
